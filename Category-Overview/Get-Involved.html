Become a Member. There are two easy ways to engage with ASME

Membership

Joining ASME is the most important connection a current or future mechanical engineer can make. ASME membership features full access to a host of career-enhancing benefits including the award-winning Mechanical Engineering magazine monthly; AccessEnginering online reference tool; ASME SmartBrief daily email news summaries; discounts on ASME technical journals, books, articles, conferences, courses and beyond. Learn more about your membership options.

Online Community Participation

Participate in ASME’s free online Community. The ASME.org Community is a social space where you can connect with your peers to stay up to date on the latest mechanical engineering news, and share your thoughts on industry topics. With the ASME.org community, ASME members and other participants can engage globally like never before. Sign up now.

How We Work

There are many ways to become involved with ASME’s volunteer groups. We have technical Divisions & Institutes in key ME industries that engage a wide range of ME experts, Interest Groups focused on emerging issues, and Sections and Student Sections so you can connect locally in your community or at your school. Learn more about ASME’s volunteer organization.
