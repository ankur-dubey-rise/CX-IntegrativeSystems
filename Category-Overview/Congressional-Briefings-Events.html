ASME Government Relations convenes congressional briefings to inform Members of Congress and their staff about the science, engineering and technology aspects of public policy issues aligned with ASME's Technology Strategy. ASME member volunteers and other stakeholders with technical expertise participate as speakers, providing an opportunity to highlight science and engineering public policy issues and increase awareness of ASME as a credible, non-partisan source of technical information.
