ASME's Strategy represents the future of the Society. Our five core technologies – Robotics, Bioengineering, Clean Energy, Manufacturing, Pressure Technology – allows ASME to focus on how the latest innovations and technologies can provide opportunities for engineers and to improve the quality of life. This focus on technology will enable ASME to be vibrant and relevant well into the 21st century.
