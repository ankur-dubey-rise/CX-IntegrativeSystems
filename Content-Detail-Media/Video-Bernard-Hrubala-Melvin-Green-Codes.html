Bernard E. Hrubala was awarded the Melvin R. Green Codes and Standards Medal for exceptional leadership in ASME Standards and Certification, particularly the advancement and promulgation of the Society’s Conformity Assessment programs, the global acceptance of ASME codes and standards, and the implementation of initiatives that enhance ASME’s position as a world renowned standards development organization.
