Pat Phelan is a professor of mechanical and aerospace engineering at Arizona State University. As program manager for emerging technologies at the U.S. Department of Energy, Phelan works with research and industry partners to develop cost-effective, energy-saving solutions, systems, and products for buildings. In this interview recorded at ASME’s Power & Energy Conference, he talks about emerging technologies in smart buildings.
