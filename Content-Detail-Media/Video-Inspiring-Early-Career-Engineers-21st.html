In this talk, Dr. Julio Guerrero, Mechanical Engineer, explains how life is beautiful and as engineers the profession makes society (the human race) much better by evolving civilization by creating; touching on how he came to be inspired to pursue engineering and introducing the concept of ICR; Innovation, Culture and Risk, and why it’s important to embrace in one's career to be successful.
