Nanotechnology is helping scientists understand and treat cancer better. Dr. Dennis Discher, the Robert D. Bent Chair Professor and Director of the NCI-funded Physical Sciences Oncology Center at the University of Pennsylvania, discusses how physical changes of tissues and cells contribute to cancer growth and initiation. This podcast was recorded at ASME’s 2016 NEMB Conference.
