How it Works

Each participating college or university submits the names of all of their eligible engineering students in one batch and makes a single payment for all - and we do the rest (you need a minimum of 10 students to participate).

Why participate? ASME Student Group Membership is a win-win for you and your students! Participating in the ASME Student Group Membership Program has many advantages for both you and your students. Here are just a few:

  • A streamlined administrative process
  • The convenience of making a single payment
  • The satisfaction knowing all of your engineering students have access to all of the valuable tools ASME has to offer including:
    • ASME SmartBrief email engineering news digests
    • AccessEngineering online reference tool
    • Mechanical Engineering magazine
    • Networking opportunities in person and online
    • Career Center + Job Board
    • Mentoring Services
    • Meetings and Events
    • Member discounts on ASME products, services and events, and much more!

If your school is interested in participating, the first step is for an individual in a leadership role at your school to download and submit our brief Student Group Membership Participation Request Form, and an ASME representative will contact them promptly.
