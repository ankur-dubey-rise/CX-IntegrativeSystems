The ASME Innovative Additive Manufacturing 3D (IAM3D) Challenge, which is part of ASME’s Global E-Fests (Engineering Festivals), is designed to give mechanical and multi-disciplinary undergraduate students around the world an opportunity to re-engineer existing products or create new designs. Students competing in ASME IAM3D will showcase their creativity by demonstrating the value added through their ingenuity, application of sound engineering design principles, and leveraging Additive Manufacturing technology to address a broad spectrum of industriala, manufacturing, and humanitarian challenges.
