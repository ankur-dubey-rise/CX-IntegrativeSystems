A Global Competition for Hardware Led Social Innovation

The ASME Innovation showcase (ISHOW) is a global competition for hardware led ventures. We focus on the design & engineering journey of taking physical products to market. We care about “social innovation”, that is, solving social and environmental issues through enterprise. We host ISHOW events in Africa, India and the USA and accept submissions globally. Winners share cash prizes and design & engineering support. We are a global network of engineers, makers, dreamers, designers, investors and entrepreneurs. Join us.
