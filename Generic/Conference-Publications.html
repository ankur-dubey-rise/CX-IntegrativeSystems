ASME Conference Publications

ASME sponsors approximately 30 conferences per year and publishes about 100 proceedings volumes annually, encompassing the entire spectrum of topics of interest to mechanical engineers and associated industries. ASME Conference Proceedings are available in print and/or in digital format through the ASME Digital Collection.

Individual conference papers from 2002 to the present are available through the ASME Digital Collection.
